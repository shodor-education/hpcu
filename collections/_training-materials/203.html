--- title: "Simple Stormwater Runoff Model for Geosciences Education" submitter: "Yuan Zhang" submitter-institution: "Ohio Supercomputer Center" submission-date: "2015-11-18" description: "This web application is designed to support education in stormwater runoff by providing a straightforward interface and simple model. Users will enter information about a watershed, land use within the watershed, and a precipitation event. The web application will output a hydrograph showing the volume of water flowing out of the drainage over time. Users can select from various units of measure and the output display. The model is based on the NRCS curve number and unit hydrograph approach." materials: - path: "http://research.bpcrc.osu.edu/resources/water_runoff_model/" description: "Model " - path: "ftp://ftp.bpcrc.osu.edu/downloads/outreach/Watersheds/30_Schoolyard_Watershed.zip" description: "Documentation and exercise" ---
